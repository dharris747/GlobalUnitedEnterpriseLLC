How to Order

We support purchase orders (POs), government purchase cards (GPC), and invoicing with standard Net 15–30 terms.

Purchase Orders

  • Email your PO to derrickharris@globalunitedent.com
  • Include scope, period of performance, POP start date, and funding reference
  • We return an acknowledgment and kickoff checklist

Invoice & Terms

  • Standard terms: Net 30 (negotiable)
  • Invoice delivery: PDF via email
  • Remit via ACH/Wire; GPC accepted with receipt

Required Docs

  • W‑9 available upon request
  • Capabilities Statement (see Capabilities)
  • UEI/CAGE & NAICS listed on Capabilities page
